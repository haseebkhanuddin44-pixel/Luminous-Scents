Shipping Details

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

During peak seasons (holidays, sales events), processing time may extend to 2-3 business days.

Domestic Shipping: We ship to all 50 US states, including Alaska and Hawaii.

International Shipping: Currently available to Canada, UK, and Australia. International shipping rates vary by location.

PO Boxes: We can ship to PO Boxes using USPS services only.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

You can track your package using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email.

For any tracking issues, please contact our customer service team.

Return Policy

Eligible Items
  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Candles must be unburned with wicks untrimmed
  • Original packaging and labels must be intact
  • Gift sets must be complete with all items included
Non-Returnable Items
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Sale items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery
How to Return
  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return
  2. Receive your return authorization and prepaid shipping label
  3. Package items securely in original packaging
  4. Attach the prepaid label and drop off at any UPS location
  5. Receive confirmation once we process your return
Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size, color, or fragrance. Exchanges follow the same process as returns, and we'll ship your new item once we receive the original.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.
