Our Story

Crafting moments of tranquility and beauty through the art of premium candle making

The Lumière Story

Founded in 2018, Lumière Candles began as a passion project in a small studio apartment. Our founder, inspired by the transformative power of scent and light, set out to create candles that would elevate everyday moments into extraordinary experiences.

What started with a simple desire to fill spaces with beautiful fragrance has grown into a commitment to sustainable luxury. Every candle we create tells a story of craftsmanship, quality, and care for both our customers and our planet.

Today, Lumière Candles is proud to be a certified B-Corporation, balancing profit with purpose as we continue to illuminate homes and hearts around the world.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We use only natural, renewable soy wax and sustainable packaging materials. Our commitment to the environment is reflected in every aspect of our business.

Quality

Each candle is hand-poured with meticulous attention to detail. We source the finest fragrance oils and materials to ensure every product meets our exacting standards.

Community

We believe in supporting local artisans and giving back to our community. A portion of every purchase supports local environmental initiatives.

Our Process

From concept to your home

1
Fragrance Development

Our perfumers carefully blend premium oils to create unique, memorable scents.

2
Hand Pouring

Each candle is individually hand-poured using natural soy wax and cotton wicks.

3
Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing for burn quality, scent throw, and safety.

4
Sustainable Packaging

Carefully packaged in recyclable materials and shipped carbon-neutral to your door.
