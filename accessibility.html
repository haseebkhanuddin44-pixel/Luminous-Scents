Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making our website accessible to everyone

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Our Commitment

At Lumière Candles, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people with disabilities. We believe that everyone should have equal access to information and functionality, and we strive to provide an inclusive online experience for all our customers.

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility and usability of our website and ensure that it meets or exceeds applicable standards.

Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including:

  • Visual impairments (including blindness, low vision, and color blindness)
  • Hearing impairments
  • Motor/mobility impairments
  • Cognitive impairments

Accessibility Features

Our website includes several accessibility features to enhance usability:

Navigation and Structure

  • Logical heading structure for screen readers
  • Skip navigation links to main content
  • Consistent navigation throughout the site
  • Breadcrumb navigation on product and category pages
  • Clear page titles and descriptions

Visual Design

  • High contrast color scheme for better readability
  • Scalable text that can be enlarged up to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Clear visual focus indicators for keyboard navigation
  • Alternative text for all meaningful images
  • Icons accompanied by descriptive text

Keyboard Navigation

  • Full keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements
  • Logical tab order throughout the site
  • Visible focus indicators
  • Keyboard shortcuts for common actions

Screen Reader Support

  • Proper use of ARIA labels and descriptions
  • Semantic HTML markup
  • Form labels and error messages
  • Status updates and notifications

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Voice recognition software
  • Keyboard navigation tools
  • Screen magnification software
  • Switch navigation devices

Browser and Device Support

Our website is tested and optimized for accessibility across:

Browsers

  • Chrome (latest version)
  • Firefox (latest version)
  • Safari (latest version)
  • Microsoft Edge (latest version)

Devices

  • Desktop computers
  • Laptops
  • Tablets
  • Mobile phones

Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to continuous improvement and regularly:

  • Conduct accessibility audits and testing
  • Train our development team on accessibility best practices
  • Review and update our accessibility features
  • Test with real users, including those who use assistive technologies
  • Stay current with accessibility standards and guidelines

Third-Party Content

While we strive to ensure that all content on our website is accessible, some third-party content (such as embedded videos, maps, or social media widgets) may not fully meet accessibility standards. We work with our third-party providers to improve accessibility where possible.

Alternative Access Methods

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website, we offer alternative ways to access our products and services:

  • Phone Orders: Call us at 1-800-LUMIERE to place orders or get product information
  • Email Support: Contact us at accessibility@lumierecandles.com for assistance
  • In-Store Shopping: Visit one of our retail locations for personalized assistance
  • Catalog Requests: Request a printed catalog with product information

Accessibility Tips

Here are some tips to help you navigate our website more effectively:

Keyboard Navigation

  • Use Tab to move forward through links and form elements
  • Use Shift + Tab to move backward
  • Use Enter or Space to activate buttons and links
  • Use arrow keys to navigate within menus and carousels

Screen Reader Users

  • Use heading navigation to quickly move between sections
  • Look for landmark regions (main, navigation, search)
  • Form fields have descriptive labels and error messages
  • Product information is structured for easy scanning

Visual Adjustments

  • Zoom your browser up to 200% for larger text
  • Use high contrast mode in your operating system
  • Adjust your browser's font size settings
  • Use browser extensions for additional customization

Legal Information

This accessibility statement applies to the Lumière Candles website (lumierecandles.com). We are committed to compliance with applicable accessibility laws and regulations, including the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.

This statement was prepared on December 1, 2024, and is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect our ongoing accessibility improvements.
