Ingredients

Our candles are made with premium natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and carefully selected fragrance oils.

  • Wax: 100% Natural Soy Wax
  • Wick: Cotton Core Wick
  • Fragrance: Premium Fragrance Oils
  • Container: Reusable Glass Vessel

All ingredients are ethically sourced and eco-friendly.

Candle Care Instructions
First Burn

Allow candle to burn for 2-3 hours on first use to create an even wax pool.

Trim the Wick

Trim wick to 1/4" before each use to prevent smoking and ensure clean burning.

Burn Time

Do not burn for more than 4 hours at a time. Allow to cool before relighting.

Safety

Never leave a burning candle unattended. Keep away from children and pets.
